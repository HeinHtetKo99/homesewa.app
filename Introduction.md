**Home Sewa** is Nepal’s **superfast, on-demand home service platform**, designed to instantly connect customers with trusted, verified professionals nearby. Powered by advanced AI-driven technology, Home Sewa ensures that service requests are matched with the right professionals quickly and efficiently.

By leveraging **artificial intelligence Home Sewa offers real-time push notifications, automated voice calls, SMS and WhatsApp alerts, location-based technology** & few other technologies to automate. Home Sewa makes booking reliable home services fast, simple, and convenient. At the same time, it empowers local professionals to maximize their earnings by connecting directly with customers, eliminating the need to pay hefty commissions to third-party intermediaries.

Our mission is to make local services **accessible, reliable, and superfast** by building a technology-driven ecosystem that creates value for both customers and service professionals.

For customers, Home Sewa offers quick access to nearby, verified, and insured professionals without middlemen, hidden charges, or commission cuts. For service providers, it provides affordable access to high-quality local leads through a Pay-Per-Lead model, creating a scalable and sustainable marketplace for everyone involved.

Built on open-source technologies, Home Sewa bridges the gap between customers and skilled professionals, making it easier than ever to find trusted local services.

Professionals can connect directly with customers by paying a nominal lead fee, typically as low as **NPR 49 or NPR 99 per lead** ensuring fair opportunities and better income potential.

As an AI-driven platform, Home Sewa goes beyond lead generation by offering additional value-added services, including **professional training, insurance support, documentation assistance, legal guidance, compliance support, and enhanced accountability mechanisms** to foster trust and transparency between both parties.

**Home Sewa is transforming local services in Nepal by making them faster, smarter, and more accessible for everyone.**

## **Mission**

To simplify access to reliable home services by leveraging AI, real-time technology, and trusted local professionals, delivering fast, convenient, and transparent solutions while empowering service providers to increase their income and grow sustainably.

## **Vision**

To become Nepal's most trusted and innovative on-demand home service platform, transforming how households connect with skilled professionals through AI based technology, automation, and exceptional customer experiences with no middleman in between. 

## **Goals**

1. Provide fast, reliable, and hassle-free home services across Nepal.  
2. Build a nationwide network of verified and skilled service professionals.  
3. Utilize AI and automation to optimize service matching and customer satisfaction.  
4. Empower local professionals by providing fair earning opportunities and reducing dependency on intermediaries.  
5. Establish Home Sewa as the leading digital marketplace for home services in Nepal.  
6. Continuously improve service quality, transparency, and customer trust.

## **Objectives**

### **Long-Term Objectives (3–5 Years)**

* Introduce advanced AI features for predictive scheduling and personalized service recommendations.

* Ensure service reliability and transparency.  
* Minimize customer wait times through intelligent matching algorithms.
